Buttered Toast

Basic Breakfast

Perfectly browned with melted butter

Ingredients

  • White sliced bread
  • Lurpack slightly salted butter

Method

  1. Ensure toaster is at a good setting for making bread brown
  2. Place in as many slices of bread as your toaster holds
  3. Plunge bread down amongst the heating elements
  4. If bread slices significantly peek above the toaster, be prepared to turn bread over half way
  5. When toaster has popped, take one slice and quickly butter
  6. Place the next slice on top and repeat until all toast is buttered
  7. Cut in half width ways
  8. Nom
