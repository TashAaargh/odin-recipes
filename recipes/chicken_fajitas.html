Chicken Fajitas

Sweet and Spicy

A quick dinner everyone can assemble themselves

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 sweet pepper, sliced
  • 8 tortilla wraps
  • Fajita Seasoning
  • 1 tbsp oil

Method

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan on high heat
  2. Fry onion and peppers until starting to brown
  3. Turn down heat to medium and add chicken
  4. Fry everything together, turning freaquently until chicken is nearly cooked
  5. Add fajita seasoning until chicken and vegetables are coated
  6. Continue frying until everything is well combined and caramellised
  7. Heat tortillas in microwave for 30 seconds
  8. Serve with nachos
