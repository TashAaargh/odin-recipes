Fish Finger Sandwich

The Perfect Lunch

Easy, quick and reminiscent of your mum's after school tea. The soft buttered bread and crunchy breadcrumbs compliment the flaky chewy fish

Ingredients

  • Fresh soft bread
  • Lurpack Spreadable butter
  • Birdseye 100% fillet omega 3 fish fingers
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Ensure the oven's wire shelf is on the uppermost rung of your oven
  2. Preheat the oven to 220 CÂ°
  3. Once at temperature, place 4 fish fingers onto a baking tray, and bake for 15 minutes or until they are golden brown and the edges are starting to blacken
  4. Take out of the oven to cool slightly
  5. Butter two slices of bread
  6. Arrange fish fingers between the buttered bread slices
  7. Apply a slight squish
  8. Cut sandwich in half
  9. Salt, or don't. I'm not your mum
